Our Mission

Our mission is to expose students of Edison High School to robotics and technology.

Team Eagle Tech is a FIRST Robotics Competition participant, in which teams design a machine capable of completing a specific challenge. While building such a robot, our team consists of capable students, mentors, and members passionate about robotics. In previous years, our team has participated in the FIRST Tech Challenge in other schools.
